# EcoExtract – Trust-Based Circular E-Waste Exchange Platform

## Secure. Reuse. Recover. Sustain.

EcoExtract is a concept-driven circular e-waste exchange platform designed to transform India’s informal electronic waste ecosystem into a secure, transparent, and sustainable digital marketplace.

The platform connects:
- Individual sellers
- Repair shops
- Authorized recyclers

through a structured workflow focused on:
- secure electronics disposal
- reusable component recovery
- responsible recycling
- circular economy integration

---

# Problem Statement

India is one of the world’s largest e-waste generators, producing millions of tonnes of discarded electronics every year. However, a large share of this waste still flows through informal scrap markets that lack:

- Secure data deletion verification
- Fair and transparent pricing
- Traceable recycling processes
- Quality assurance for reusable parts
- Environmentally safe disposal practices

This creates major risks including:
- data theft
- unfair valuation
- toxic disposal
- loss of reusable electronic value

> “The market does not lack e-waste — it lacks trust, transparency, and structure.”

---

# Our Solution

EcoExtract creates a trusted circular marketplace where users can:

- Sell old electronics securely
- Receive AI-assisted fair pricing
- Negotiate transparently with human evaluators
- Schedule doorstep pickup
- Obtain certified data destruction proof
- Purchase tested reusable spare parts
- Support responsible mineral recovery

The platform integrates resale, reuse, refurbishment, and recycling into one connected ecosystem.
